Kubeflow Ep2: Pipeline & Component

Overview

In episode 1, I have introduced to you guys the first steps of approaching Kubeflow in the easy way along with some basic examples. You probably had some fun with it right? Now everything is all set, let’s go a bit deeper into the most interesting part of the series - how to produce your own components and pipelines?

Component

The term component here refers to a task (or step) in your ML workflow which is able to contain your code and perform its role per se. It could be a preprocessing task, training a model or even postprocessing output. Kubeflow aims to satisfy the Pythonista who want to simplify the stages of making ML workflow without any effort but efficiently. Therefore, the way of defining component should be easy for us to follow. In addition, it also supports us by more than just an option when you need more customizations.
On the other hand, we will figure out how the data passes through components in a pipeline as well as how to utilize Component Store to load shared components.
